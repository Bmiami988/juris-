About Lexi

Democratizing legal knowledge for every Ugandan citizen

Our Mission

Lexi was born from a simple but powerful idea: everyone deserves access to understandable legal information. In Uganda, navigating the legal system can be complex, expensive, and intimidating. We're using artificial intelligence to break down these barriers, providing instant, accurate, and free legal guidance to all Ugandans.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Lexi free to use?

Yes! Lexi is completely free. We believe legal information should be accessible to everyone without barriers.

Is my conversation private?

Absolutely. Your chat sessions are encrypted and we don't store personal data. Each session uses a unique ID that doesn't identify you personally.

Can Lexi replace a lawyer?

No. Lexi provides legal information and general guidance, but it is not a substitute for professional legal advice from a qualified attorney. For complex legal matters, always consult a lawyer.

What legal sources does Lexi use?

Lexi draws from the Ugandan Constitution, The Penal Code Act, The Land Act, The Employment Act, The Children Act, and other statutory laws, plus legal commentary and resources.

⚠️ Important Disclaimer: Lexi is an AI-powered informational tool. While we strive for accuracy, laws change and interpretations vary. Always verify critical legal information with a qualified legal professional. Lexi does not create an attorney-client relationship.
